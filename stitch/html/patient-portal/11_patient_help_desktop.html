Frequently Asked Questions

When will my lab results be ready?

expand_more

Most routine blood test results are available within 24 to 48 hours. Specialized or complex tests may take 3 to 5 business days. You will receive an email or SMS notification (based on your preferences) as soon as new results are uploaded to your portal.

How do I interpret my test results?

expand_more

Your results are displayed alongside standard reference ranges. If a value is outside the normal range, it will be highlighted. We strongly recommend discussing all results with your referring healthcare provider, as they can interpret them in the context of your personal medical history.

Can I download or print my reports?

expand_more

Yes. Navigate to the 'Reports' section from the side menu. Click on the specific report you wish to view, and select the 'Download PDF' or 'Print' icon located at the top right of the document viewer.

Who can see my records?

expand_more

Your privacy is our priority. Your records are only accessible to you, the referring physician, and authorized clinical staff at Lumina Path. You can manage access permissions for specific providers in the 'Settings' menu under 'Privacy & Sharing'.
